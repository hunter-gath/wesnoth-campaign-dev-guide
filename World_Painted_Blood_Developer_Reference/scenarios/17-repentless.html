17 — Repentless

The cabal's outer fortress is defended by living soldiers who remain awake inside magical compulsion. The Hunter must continue forward without confusing relentless purpose with indiscriminate cruelty. Three ward rings protect Magister Vorn and Saedra. Each can be opened by a key, a control circle, or a living sacrifice, allowing the player's established moral method to become a direct tactical choice.

Moral decision

ChoiceImmediate effectPersistent state
Free and arm themGeneral Jalen Rusk joins Side 1 as a loyal unit.wpb_rescued +1, wpb_conscripted +1, wpb_alliance_strength +1
Use them as baitThe captive is killed; the principal boss is harmed and Side 1 receives immediate gold.wpb_sacrificed +1, wpb_corruption +1

Only the explicit choice event changes sacrifice and corruption. Ordinary battlefield losses do not automatically count as deliberate sacrifices.
