05 — At Dawn They Sleep

A false dawn shines through crystals above a crypt packed with sleeping bats and blood-drinking undead. The light does not destroy them; it merely holds them inside their dreams. Before the crystals dim, the Hunter must destroy the blood cisterns and kill Seryn and Vaska. Captives chained beside the controls can maintain the light—or be abandoned to occupy the brood.

Only the explicit choice event changes sacrifice and corruption. Ordinary battlefield losses do not automatically count as deliberate sacrifices.

Lessons for authors

Design interpretation. These are reusable readings of the source, not claims explicitly written in WML.

PatternReuse lesson
Artificial dawn themeNarrative lighting can establish a unique crypt identity even with the standard underground schedule.
Flying enemy mixBats change how players value corridors and chokepoints.
Two-leader hierarchySeparate brood and necromancer leadership for tactical texture.
