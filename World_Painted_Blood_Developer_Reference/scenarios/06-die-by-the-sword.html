06 — Die by the Sword

The blood shipments lead to a mining settlement where living laborers work until death and continue their shifts after being raised. Sir Malrec claims the sword is the only law the helpless understand. The Hunter must capture the rail junctions, destroy the corpse-processing line, and take Malrec's ledger. The miners can be evacuated, armed, or ordered to hold lethal side tunnels.

Moral decision

ChoiceImmediate effectPersistent state
Free and arm themAldren Vale joins Side 1 as a loyal unit.wpb_rescued +1, wpb_conscripted +1, wpb_alliance_strength +1
Use them as baitThe captive is killed; the principal boss is harmed and Side 1 receives immediate gold.wpb_sacrificed +1, wpb_corruption +1

Only the explicit choice event changes sacrifice and corruption. Ordinary battlefield losses do not automatically count as deliberate sacrifices.
