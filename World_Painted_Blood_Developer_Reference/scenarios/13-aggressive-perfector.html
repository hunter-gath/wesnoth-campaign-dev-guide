13 — Aggressive Perfector

A hidden laboratory attempts to combine the strongest traits of living and undead bodies. Its failed creations fill the cells, while a perfected copy of the Hunter waits in the central chamber. The Hunter must disable the vats, defeat the imitation built from his choices, and destroy Sileth's archive. Every vat can be opened carefully or shattered with whoever remains inside.

Moral decision

ChoiceImmediate effectPersistent state
Free and arm themKessa Thorn joins Side 1 as a loyal unit.wpb_rescued +1, wpb_conscripted +1, wpb_alliance_strength +1
Use them as baitThe captive is killed; the principal boss is harmed and Side 1 receives immediate gold.wpb_sacrificed +1, wpb_corruption +1

Only the explicit choice event changes sacrifice and corruption. Ordinary battlefield losses do not automatically count as deliberate sacrifices.
