11 — Fictional Reality

The next cavern contains a human city beneath a bright false sky. Its people believe the surface has fallen and that Provost Caldrin alone protects them from staged undead attacks. The Hunter must gather evidence, reveal the corpse warehouses, and overthrow Caldrin and his necromantic partner Morrowmask. Destroying the illusion forces the population to confront the cavern walls around them.

Moral decision

ChoiceImmediate effectPersistent state
Free and arm themVeyla Sorn joins Side 1 as a loyal unit.wpb_rescued +1, wpb_conscripted +1, wpb_alliance_strength +1
Use them as baitThe captive is killed; the principal boss is harmed and Side 1 receives immediate gold.wpb_sacrificed +1, wpb_corruption +1

Only the explicit choice event changes sacrifice and corruption. Ordinary battlefield losses do not automatically count as deliberate sacrifices.
