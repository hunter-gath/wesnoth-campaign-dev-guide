Why only 25 files?

The full guide analyzes the complete campaign source surface. This smaller snapshot exists for one specific reproducible calculation: the custom-unit transitive dependency and inherited-audio closure. It includes:

  • all 14 campaign unit definitions,
  • the campaign’s ABILITY_DISTRACT definition,
  • the two inherited core unit types, and
  • the core macro files that define every symbol reached by those units.

Machine-readable provenance

The full manifest is bundled at assets/source-snapshot.json. The archive does not redistribute the upstream WML snapshot itself; it records paths, pinned links, byte counts, and hashes.
