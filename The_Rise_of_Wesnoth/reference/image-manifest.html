Production concentration

FamilyFilesInterpretation
Noble line26 sprite/animation framesHaldric’s four-level line carries the largest bespoke human animation investment.
Wesfolk line50 framesThree levels, masked and unmasked presentations, staff/sling attacks, defense, and leadership.
Jevyan12 framesSeparate cloaked, standing, melee, defense, and magical presentation.
Familiar5 framesSmall custom unit animation set paired with the campaign’s only local sound.
Vampire Lady8 framesOne-scenario boss receives a complete small combat set.
Asset strategy. Bespoke unit art is concentrated on Haldric, Jessene, Jevyan, and a few signature characters. Ordinary armies, terrain, scenery, items, ships, and most effects are supplied by core Wesnoth.
