Catalogue scope

Haldric’s Noble line

Noble Youth (L0) 24 HP · 5 move · 4×2 impact │ 12 XP ▼ Noble Fighter (L1) 36 HP · 6 move · 7×3 blade │ 40 XP ▼ Noble Commander (L2) 48 HP · leadership · 8×4 sword · 6×3 bow │ 90 XP ▼ Noble Lord (L3) 64 HP · leadership · 11×4 sword · 8×3 bow · AMLA

The Ruby of Fire is intentionally not baked into any type. Scenario 12 adds it as a permanent object, and Scenario 19 removes that object again.

Jessene’s Wesfolk line

Wesfolk Outcast (L1) 33 HP · 7 move · leadership + distract │ 40 XP ▼ Wesfolk Lady (L2) 46 HP · 7 move · leadership + distract │ 80 XP ▼ Wesfolk Leader (L3) 62 HP · 7 move · leadership + distract · AMLA

Every level has masked and unmasked visual variants. The underlying combat identity remains the same while Scenario 07 reconstructs Lady Outlaw as Lady Jessene with the unmasked variation.
