Design reading

The first three scenarios move from a linear opening into two different kinds of agency:

Agency typeScenarioWhat changes
Party/roster agencyThe FallCompanion presence, recruit list, and immediate gold
Route agencyA Harrowing EscapeNext map, environment, and story chapter
Design interpretation. The campaign waits until the player understands its central conflict before offering branches. The choices therefore modify an established journey instead of competing with the initial premise.
