Inheritance caveat

Syntactic closure, not animation arbitration. Warrior King inherits Royal Warrior animations and then adds older hit/death presentation. The report retains every source dependency reached through inheritance. A later scalar such as die_sound, or competing animation scoring, may shadow an inherited reference during a particular runtime event.

This conservative rule is useful for maintenance: deleting a core sound remains visible as a possible dependency even when current animation selection usually favors the campaign override.
