From linear route to reusable hub

Before Scenario 16At Scenario 16
Most scenario transitions have one next chapter or a one-time branch.The same hub is revisited after four interchangeable branches.
Choice state is usually consumed after reconvergence.Quest flags persist until all four are complete.
Dialogue follows a fixed scenario order.Dialogue is rendered from completion count and last-completed quest.
Carryover normally represents battle economy.Hub departures preserve 100% because no battle occurs.

Hub-state separation

StateQuestion answered
num_doneHow far through the council’s overall test is Haldric?
Four quest flagsWhich destinations should still be described and displayed?
last_doneWhich lord should acknowledge the most recent victory?
Architecture lesson. These variables are intentionally nonredundant. A count cannot identify unfinished quests, while four booleans cannot cheaply select the broad first/second/third/final council response.

Transition-state lifecycle

StageState operation
Scenario 15 endingInitializes all six hub variables.
Quest selection in Scenario 16Reads flags to hide completed options; preserves all gold.
Scenario 17 completionExpected to set its flag, increment count, and set last_done.
Scenario 16 final visitClears all six variables and advances permanently.
