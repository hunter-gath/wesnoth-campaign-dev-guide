Objective evolution

ScenarioRequired successOptional or graded success
08Board any available ship or defeat all leadersFour explicitly labeled moral/tactical grades
09Get the book and have Haldric enter the sewerDefeat all leaders for bonus
10Have Haldric leave the sewerDefeat both wizards before leaving
Design interpretation. The arc repeatedly asks how much danger the player will accept before escaping. It changes the physical form—ships, mine entrance, sewer exit—while retaining the same strategic question.

Three kinds of pressure

08 — Calendar pressure

Ships arrive and depart on fixed turns while winter threatens the port.

09 — Activation pressure

The player chooses when to add a petrified undead faction to an existing orc battle.

10 — Navigation pressure

Shroud and a blocked central channel make route knowledge part of the reward economy.

The arc demonstrates three increasingly economical approaches: explicit AI state, local quest flags, and direct queries against current unit state.
