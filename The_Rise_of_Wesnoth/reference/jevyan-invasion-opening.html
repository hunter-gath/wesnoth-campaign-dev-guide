Artifact concealment as mechanics

12 A Final Spring └── Haldric gains object_id = ruby_of_fire 19 The Vanguard ├── [remove_object] deletes ruby_of_fire from Haldric ├── Jessene leaves with the artifact └── Lady Jessene stored in jessica_store 20 Return of the Fleet ├── Jevyan observes that the magical signal is concealed └── Jessene returns, but Haldric's Ruby attack is not restored here
Engineering lesson. The story does not merely claim the Ruby is elsewhere. The unit carrying its attack stops having that attack.
