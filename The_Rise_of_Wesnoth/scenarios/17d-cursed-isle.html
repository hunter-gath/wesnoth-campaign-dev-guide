Story and premise

Haldric travels by sea to the fog-covered Isle of Tears, where the long-dead members of an elven clan remain cursed. Jessene distinguishes these ancient elf-dead from the more utilitarian undead once used by the Wesfolk. Minister Edren treats the mission as a holy purge.

Two Spectre leaders openly command the island. A Vampire Lady called the Midnight Queen is hidden in one of three central temples and appears only if the player enters her assigned temple. Her awakening changes the displayed objective.

Map description

The 35 × 42 map is an elongated island surrounded by ocean, with beaches, grass, forest, swamp, villages, roads, water channels, two undead keeps, three central temples, and holy-water sites toward the southeastern approach. Fog conceals the temple identities and enemy distribution.

Random temple assignment

Three local macros generate the temple interactions:

MacroResult
TEMP_LOOT X YOne-time 50-gold pickup.
TEMP_QUEEN X YCreates the Midnight Queen, several Blood Bats, dialogue, objective refresh, and queen_awake=1.
TEMP_EMPTY X YReports an empty temple.
DifficultyThree-temple contents
EasyQueen + loot + loot
Normal / HardQueen + loot + empty

A random value from 1 to 3 rotates these roles among temples at 11,13; 10,17; and 9,15.

Midnight Queen encounter

Entering the selected temple:

  1. Creates the Midnight Queen as a Vampire Lady on side 2.
  2. Creates three Blood Bats, plus a fourth on Hard.
  3. Runs an enthrallment scene in which Jessene breaks Haldric’s fascination.
  4. Sets queen_awake=1.
  5. Refreshes objectives.

When the Queen reaches last breath, she curses the party; Haldric observes that the remaining undead are fading. The scenario kills every side-2 and side-3 undead unit without firing their death events and immediately ends with bonus victory.

Holy water and Minister Edren

The core OBJ_POTION_HOLY_ALT macro places holy water at 29,24, with a second pickup at 28,26 on Easy. Minister Edren also receives a special line whenever he kills an undead unit.

Hub-state write

{VARIABLE_OP num_done add 1}
[set_variable]
    name=cursed_isle
    value=1
[/set_variable]
[set_variable]
    name=last_done
    value="Isle"
[/set_variable]

The local random variable is cleared after assigning temples. The Queen flag is cleared when victory resolves.

Lessons for campaign authors

PatternReuse lesson
Randomize event identities among fixed landmarksPreserve map readability while changing exploration outcomes.
Change objectives when a hidden boss appearsKeep the UI synchronized with newly revealed requirements.
Make discovery optional but consequentialA player who awakens the Queen must complete the stronger objective.
Boss death dissolves the factionUse a scripted cleanup when ordinary unit-by-unit victory would weaken the narrative.
Difficulty changes resources and exploration rewardsThe extra Easy holy water and second loot temple provide help without separate maps.
