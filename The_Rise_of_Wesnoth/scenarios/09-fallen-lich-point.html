Story & dialogue

Haldric makes landfall outside Southbay to recover Lich-Lord Caror’s Book of Fire and Darkness. The battlefield already contains two orc armies and a petrified undead force. A monolith explains that only a noble of the royal line can end the spell, turning Haldric’s lineage into both story proof and a gameplay gate.

The player may free Caror immediately or delay until the orcs are weakened. Once released, the lich senses the Ruby of Fire and becomes another hostile leader. Killing him yields the book; Haldric must then be the first unit to enter the sewer.

Map description

The 37 × 47 surface map is tall and varied, with mountains and hills in the north, snowy roads and villages, forest belts, water, shore, ruined structures, a mine entrance, and a southern petrified undead formation around the monolith. Haldric lands by Transport Galleon on the eastern coast. Two orc keeps control northern and central approaches, while the sewer entrance lies at the abandoned mine near 15,9.

Petrified faction system

A local PETRIFIED macro adds petrified=yes. A second local macro creates six loyal-upkeep Bone Shooters and Revenants around the lich so their upkeep does not alter displayed side gold before activation.

#define PETRIFIED_UNIT TYPE X Y
    [unit]
        side=4
        type={TYPE}
        x={X}
        y={Y}
        {PETRIFIED}
        upkeep=loyal
    [/unit]
#enddef

When Haldric chooses the royal phrase:

  1. lich_free becomes 1.
  2. [unpetrify] activates the petrified side.
  3. Side-4 non-leader units are changed to full upkeep.
  4. Side 4 receives normal zero income.
  5. Caror and the party exchange Ruby-focused dialogue.
Design advantage. The enemy force is visible and strategically predictable, but the player controls when it becomes active.

Why the book flag is temporary

have_book is needed only to gate the sewer exit while this scenario is running. Every successful outcome guarantees that the Book of Fire and Darkness was obtained, so the flag is cleared during scenario_end. Later dialogue can treat book possession as an invariant.
